Solving real-world water challenges

The Water Institute works with individuals and organizations interested in solving water challenges here in North Carolina and around the world. We bring together experts from across the University in partnership with our sponsors, to review the latest science, test hypotheses, collect and analyze data and then together, use the data for making decisions and strengthening policies and practice. But we don’t stop here. Together we share the data and lessons learned so others might benefit from the work and change the way the world works on water.
